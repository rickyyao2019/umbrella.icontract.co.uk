WHY DO YOU NEED A COMPLIANT UMBRELLA COMPANY?

As of April 2020, the UK government will be making significant changes to its IR35 rules. The new rules will directly affect the way some contractors get paid. Under the new off-payroll rules, contractors will no longer be able to determine their own IR35 status. Instead, the status will be determined by the end-client. This means that, when you apply for a new contract that’s considered within IR35, you will need to be registered with an Umbrella Company.

Once registered you will not only be fully IR35 compliant, you’ll also be able to take advantage of benefits including:

  • Comprehensive Business Insurance
  • Holiday & Statutory Benefits such as SSP, SMP, SAP etc.
  • Contributory Pension Scheme via Auto-Enrolment
  • Access to an employee benefits scheme worth in excess of £1000 per year
